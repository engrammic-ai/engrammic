# Anthropic "Dreaming" Feature (May 2026)

## What it is

Automatic memory consolidation for Managed Agents, modeled after REM sleep. Runs as a scheduled background process.

## Mechanics

- **Triggers**: 24hrs since last consolidation OR 5+ sessions
- **Phases**: Orientation (understand current state) -> Consolidation (merge/prune) -> Output (new memory store for review)
- **Cross-agent**: Surfaces patterns across multiple agents (recurring mistakes, converged workflows, shared preferences)

## Availability

Research preview, Managed Agents only. Developers must request access.

## Delta Prime positioning

**Overlap**: Both address "memory for agents" problem space. Validates the market.

**Differentiation**:
- Dreaming = post-hoc pattern extraction and consolidation
- Delta Prime = live belief state management during reasoning (WorkingBelief, confidence, contradictions, supersession)
- We provide epistemic structure; they provide consolidation
- Provenance and time-travel are unique to us

**Potential relationship**: Complementary. Their consolidation could feed into our epistemic layer.

**Risk**: If dreaming ships broadly to all Claude users, "memory for agents" becomes table stakes. Our moat is the epistemology, not the storage.
